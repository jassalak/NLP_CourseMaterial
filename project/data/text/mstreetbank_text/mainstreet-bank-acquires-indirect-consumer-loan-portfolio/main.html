EagleBank acquired the indirect consumer loan portfolio in a merger with Virginia Heritage Bank during the fourth quarter of 2014. During the transition, the team that originated the indirect consumer loan portfolio at Virginia Heritage Bank joined with MainStreet Bank. Total assets for MainStreet Bank inclusive of the new portfolio approximate $455 million as of July 24, 2015 and the bank remains well capitalized.
