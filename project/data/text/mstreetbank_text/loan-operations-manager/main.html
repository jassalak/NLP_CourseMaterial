Loan Operations Manager Loan Operations Manager— This position is responsible for overseeing the daily functions of the Loan Operations Department in accordance with established systems and procedures, as well as determining process improvements and efficiencies that will be recommended to the Chief Information Officer. Other responsibilities include: Oversees and directs daily loan operations functions of the Bank by determining workflow and assignments. Administers department’s functions ensuring compliance to the Bank’s policy, legal and government rules and regulations by establishing, monitoring and maintaining policies and procedures. Responsible for resolution of operational problems. Acts as a resource to other divisions regarding department operational issues. Qualified candidates must possess the following: Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience Minimum five years of relevant bank experience Superior knowledge of the industry’s processes, procedures and workflow
