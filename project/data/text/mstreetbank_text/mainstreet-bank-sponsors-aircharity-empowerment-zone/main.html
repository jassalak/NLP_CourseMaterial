MainStreet Bank Sponsors “Aircharity® Empowerment Zone” Fairfax, VA – MainStreet Bank (OTCQB: MNSB) is sponsoring the “Aircharity® Empowerment Zone” at this year’s 21st Annual Safeway Barbecue Battle. The event will be held on Historic Pennsylvania Avenue between 9th and 14th Street NW in Washington, DC on June 22nd and 23rd. “The ‘Aircharity® Empowerment Zone’ is hosting several local charities, so the public can learn about some of the great charitable organizations working in the Washington, DC metropolitan area,” said Chairman, CEO and President Jeff W. Dick. “Charities such as: Boys and Girls Clubs of Greater Washington, Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, Be the Match, Columbia Lighthouse for the Blind and Visually Impaired, The Musicianship, Boys Town and 25:40 will also be taking donations at the event using the aircharity® product.” Aircharity® is the most cost efficient way to raise money for people in need. Aircharity® is an all-in-one solution that allows an individual or organization to open an FDIC insured account which accepts debit or credit card and electronic check donations via all forms of social media including email, websites, blogs, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn and Google+.
