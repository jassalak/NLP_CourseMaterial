MainStreet Bank Reports a Profitable Second Quarter Fairfax, VA – MainStreet Bank (OTCQB: MNSB) reported a profitable second quarter with net income of $374,000. The Bank’s book value per share was $9.68 as of June 30, 2013. The closing share price as of June 30, 2013 was $7.60, which was up 63% from the June 30, 2012 closing share price. “We have initiated several measures to improve our operating efficiency, and those efficiencies should start to become apparent in our financials in the 3rd quarter,” stated Chairman, CEO & President Jeff W. Dick. “In addition we have a very solid loan portfolio, the net effect of which should result in a reasonable year of performance.” The Bank reports total assets of $265.5 million, gross loans of $223.8 million and total deposits of $238 million. The Bank remains very well capitalized. MainStreet Bank will soon offer mobile deposits through your smart phone. MainStreet now offers an expanded merchant services solution with extremely competitive pricing. Additionally, the Bank offers a proprietary fundraising solution called Aircharity®. Aircharity® gives you the ability to open an account that automatically is set to accept debit card, credit card and electronic check donations. Donations can be made through a unique tool that resides on the customer’s website, email or any other form of social media. The Bank has opened relationships for Boys Town, the American Red Cross, the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, Active Minds, Boys and Girls Clubs of Greater Washington, and many others. Please visit www.aircharity.net to learn more or to donate to one of the many charities using Aircharity®. MainStreet operates branches in Herndon, Fairfax, Fairfax City, McLean and Clarendon. In addition, MainStreet has 55,000 free ATMs and an online banking solution called “airbanking”, with the tag line, “Bank Where you Breathe”. With a robust and easy-to-use online business banking technology, MainStreet has “put our bank” in well over 500 businesses in the Metropolitan area and across the United States. The Bank is not restricted by a conventional branching system, as it can offer business customers the ability to Put Our Bank in Your Office®. MainStreet Bank was the first community bank in the Washington, DC Metropolitan area to offer a full online business banking solution. MainStreet Bank was also the first bank headquartered in the Commonwealth of Virginia to offer CDARS – a solution that provides FDIC insurance on deposits up to $50 million. Further information on the Bank can be obtained by visiting its website at mstreetbank.com.
