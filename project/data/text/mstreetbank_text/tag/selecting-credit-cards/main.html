Tag Archives: selecting credit cards With a million (or what seems to be a million) choices to choose from, selecting a credit card can be a hard decision and not one you want to take lightly. Here are a few pointers to help you decide which card is best for you. Investigate rates- Compare Annual Percentage Rates (APRs) to find the lowest for you. Remember most cards are advertised “as low as” and the rate you’ll qualify for is usually dependent on your credit score. Also be aware of introductory rates that shoot up astronomically after the first 6-12 months, and penalty rates that might be out of control…
