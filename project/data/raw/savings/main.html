Reward Information: When your Kasasa Cash or Kasasa Cash Back account qualifications are met during a Monthly Qualification Cycle, the following rewards will be distributed to your account(s) on the last day of the statement cycle: Kasasa Saver: Balances up to $15,000.00 receive APY of 0.75%; and balances over $15,000.00 earn 0.25% interest rate on the portion of balance over $15,000.00, resulting in a range from 0.25% to 0.75% APY depending on the account’s balance. Kasasa Cash Back: 2.00% cash back on up to a total of $400.00 PIN-based/signature based debit card purchases that post and settle to the account during that cycle period. Kasasa Cash: All balances earn 0.05 APY*. Kasasa Cash Back: No cash back payments are made. When linked to the Kasasa Saver account, the interest earned within the Kasasa Cash account does not compound since it is automatically transferred to the Kasasa Saver account the day after being credited to your Kasasa Cash account.
